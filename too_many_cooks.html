Too Many Cooks

Too Many Cooks is a goofy and apocalyptic reimagination of the popular cook-and-serve game Overcooked. Survival depends on serving food to cannibalistic zombies who are invading your kitchen.

Notes

Too Many Cooks is inspired by Adult Swim’s viral video of the same name, and was based on the game mechanics of Overcooked!, a popular video game series by Team17.

As a final project submission for a student-run course CS198: Game Development and Design DeCal, the game was also presented alongside others during GDD's Spring 2019 Final Showcase.
