Innovative Design

Branding is essential to effective marketing, but not all organizations are blessed with an internal designer to fulfill that role. As a pro-bono design consultant under InnoD, I created logos for two UC Berkeley-affiliated clubs.

HAAS Consulting The Magic Club

Haas Consulting Club

The Haas Consulting Club (HCC) is a student-led organization that serves as a key resource for Haas MBA students considering a career in consulting. HCCs mission is to provide students with the resources, training, and connections to Haas staff, alumni, and current students with experience in consulting that will help the HCC members explore the consulting space and assist them with finding a job in the consulting industry.

Symbolism

With the club's mission to drive business students to succeed in their careers, the paper plane symbolizes ideation, communication, and the motion to fly upwards. The plane is colored in blue and gold to represent UC Berkeley. These colors are then divided to form the letter "h" (for Haas) in a triangular shape.

The Magic Club

The Magic Club at Berkeley shares their love and joy for magic through performing various tricks like cardistry around campus.

The client requested a “gothic and edgy” design that uses elements of the card deck (the four suits and the colors red, gold, white and black), which should be easily transferable to black and white.
