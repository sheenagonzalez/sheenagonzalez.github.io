AZLyrics

This modern redesign of AZLyrics revamps the usability and aesthetics of the once-popular lyrics website, ranking top 5 out of 37 redesigns in an intro to web development course.

PLAY WITH PROTOTYPE

Key Insights

The downfall of AZLyrics, one of the most popular websites for searching lyrics in the early 2000s, is attributed to its outdated design failing to adhere to major design principles. As a redesign exercise, this project hopes to renovate the existing product by reimagining it as a modern website. By focusing on the main function of searching and reading lyrics, AZLyrics receives a whole new, refreshing look with enhanced features.

Heuristic Evaluation

We evaluated the existing website and uncovered critical design problems.

Minimalism

From the get-go, the homepage is cluttered with unnecessary features that overshadow the main function of searching.

Efficiency of Use

Hierarchy is lacking when it comes to delivering information, leaving the user guessing where to click next.

Consistency

Parts do not align with each other, breaking the eye flow and making the information harder to digest.

Redesign

New design adds a modern feel and emphasizes function.

A more intuitive way of searching.

The search bar is made bigger and better so users can click on it right away.

Keep track as you sing along.

Lyrics are easier to read through the new typeface and highlighting function.

All with a refreshing new look.

Enhance your singing experience with a techno gradient design.
