The Art of Pana

To honor my roots as a Fil-Am artist, I took a creative spin on indigenous Filipino culture through the character design of Pana, which showcases tribal clothing, tools, activities, and Baybayin writing.

Tools

Corel Painter Essentials

Adobe Photoshop

Adobe Illustrator

Notes

Pana is created as a personal project for Innovative Design, where I was tasked with making a character and developing the narratives of their world. The project was presented for weekly feedback infront of the design team, and printed on an 8-page zine.
