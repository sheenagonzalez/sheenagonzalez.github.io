ABOUT

Sheena Gonzalez

Hi. I’m Sheena, a designer-developer focused on product, interface, and illustration. Currently, I work as a Front-end Developer Intern for Marqui Labs making websites for clients like NASA. In May 2020, I graduated with a BA in Cognitive Science from the University of California, Berkeley. There, I led the graphic design for non-profit STEM organizations, and briefly worked as a design consultant under Berkeley’s premier creative agency. In the future, I look forward to becoming a full-fledged UX Designer and fulfill my mission to integrate human-centered design to impactful technologies.

Besides being a designer, I’m a proud Filipina with a craving for all things artistic. I enjoy filling up my sketchbook or reading manga while listening to my favorite Pinoy indie-alternative bands. I also love walking my two lovely dogs, playing videogames and basketball, and binge-watching Netflix shows.
