ABOUT

Sheena Gonzalez

Hi. I’m Sheena, a designer-developer focused on interfaces, branding and illustrations. I recently graduated from University of California, Berkeley with a B.A. in Cognitive Science and a minor in Computer Science. Previously, I worked as a pro-bono graphic designer for Filipino communities in the Bay Area (PASAE, FASTERCon), and briefly under Berkeley’s premier creative agency (Innovative Design). In the near future, I’m aiming for a full-time job in UX with a mission to integrate human-centered design to innovative and impactful technologies.

Besides being a designer, I’m a proud Filipina with a passion for all things artistic. I enjoy filling up my sketchbook and getting my daily dose of Filipino music. I spend my free time relaxing with my dogs, playing games, and binge-watching Netflix shows.
