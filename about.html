ABOUT

Sheena Gonzalez

Hi, I'm Sheena and I love all things beautifully practical. As the only front-end developer at Blissy, my job is to increase market conversions by improving the online shopping experience for our customers. Upon graduating from the University of California, Berkeley in 2020, I interned for Marqui Labs, where I got the exciting opportunity to develop for NASA-affiliated sites! I then spent the following summer interning for CDA's inZaLab program, designing a web platform for GenZ creatives. I enjoy the process of building projects and seeing them come to life—be it through a canvas or a text editor. Front-end, to me, is where my interests in design and development converge in a way that positively impacts others.

Outside of work, I enjoy art as a hobby (IG@sheenaxart) and would love to make it a side business. I'm also an INFJ, anime-enthusiast, kdrama addict and dog mom of two.
